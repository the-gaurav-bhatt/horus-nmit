About

At Horus, we believe in the power of electric vehicles and their potential to shape a sustainable future. Our goal is to bring together like-minded individuals who share a passion for electric vehicles and are committed to promoting their widespread adoption. As a member of Horus, you will have the opportunity to participate in various events, workshops, and discussions focused on electric vehicles. You will also have access to exclusive resources, such as industry experts and cutting-edge technology, to help you further your knowledge and skills.
